About Me

I'm a researcher in AI and mobile robotics and currently a PhD candidate with a background in computer science and control engineering. My work centers on developing advanced perception and navigation algorithms for ground-based mobile robots, with a particular focus on semantic mapping.
I'm passionate about Robotics, Automotive, and Aerospace, and I thrive in both team environments and leadership roles. At present, I'm collaborating with Leonardo SpA and the Istituto Italiano di Tecnologia (IIT) to enhance robots’ capabilities in object detection, re-identification, localization, and mapping.
Whether it's pushing the boundaries of autonomous navigation or bridging theory with real-world applications, I’m driven by the challenge of building intelligent systems that move, sense, and understand their environments.
